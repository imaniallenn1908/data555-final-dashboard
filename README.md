# WASHFIT Dashboard Titles 
Dashboard 1: WASHFIT Scores in Diamaguène Sicap Mbao, Dakar, Senegal
Dashboard 2: Status of WASHFIT Indicators

## Overview
This project presents an interactive Shiny dashboard analyzing WASHFIT (Water, 
Sanitation, Hygiene, and Health Facility Improvement Tool) indicators across 
primary health facilities in Diamaguène Sicap Mbao, Dakar, Senegal. 
The dashboard visualizes facility-level performance across key WASH domains to 
identify gaps in service delivery.

## Research Topic
This study examines the status of water, sanitation, and hygiene infrastructure 
in health facilities and evaluates compliance with WASHFIT standards. The goal 
is to support evidence-based improvements in healthcare facility readiness and 
environmental health conditions.

## Data Source
- Data were derived from WASHFIT facility assessments conducted by Pan African
Consortium of Experts (PACE) in Dakar, Senegal.
- The dataset is confidential and used for academic purposes only.
- Data were collected through structured on-site evaluations of health facilities.

## Study Population
Primary health facilities located in the Diamaguène Sicap Mbao district of
Dakar, Senegal.

## Time Period
Data were collected between May-July 2025.

## Real-World Impact
This dashboard supports monitoring of WASHFIT performance across health facilities, 
helping identify service gaps and prioritize targeted improvements in water, 
sanitation, and hygiene infrastructure. The findings provide actionable insights
for strengthening health system readiness and improving patient care environments.
